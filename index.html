About
Friends Church Kakamega

The Kakamega Friends meeting started sometime in the late 60’s. Those who were working in the government offices and had come from various places had no place to fellowship therefore had to fellowship in other churches especially in the Anglican Church of Kenya (A.C.K) When they found out that they were a reasonable number, they started meeting in each other’s homes.
